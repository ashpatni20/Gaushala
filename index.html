About Us

Navkar Gaushala began in Dhule with a small group of devotees who believed in Ahimsa and Jeevdaya. Over five years, it has grown into a safe home for hundreds of injured and abandoned cows thanks to our caretakers, trustees, and supporters.

Our Work

Cow Rescue & Care

We rescue cows from accidents, abandonment, and cruelty. Each cow is fed, treated, and given a clean place to rest.

Feeding & Shelter

Our cows eat fresh fodder, drink clean water, and stay in large, shaded shelters with regular vet visits.

Sustainability

We protect nature by composting, reusing cow dung, and conserving water whenever possible.
